Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our practice and services. If you don't see your question here, please don't hesitate to contact us.

How do I schedule an appointment?

You can schedule an appointment by calling our office, using our online booking form, or sending us an email. We'll respond promptly to confirm your appointment time.

What should I do in case of a dental emergency?

For dental emergencies, please call our emergency line immediately at +1 (647) 558-78-56. This line is available 24/7, and we'll provide guidance and arrange for urgent care as needed.

Do you accept insurance?

Yes, we accept most major dental insurance plans. Our team will help you understand your coverage and maximize your benefits. Please bring your insurance information to your first visit.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept cash, personal checks, and all major credit cards. We also offer flexible financing options to help make your dental care affordable.

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend most patients visit every six months for a check-up and professional cleaning. However, some patients may need more frequent visits based on their oral health needs.
